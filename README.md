# SOC Analyst + GRC Career Roadmap

A zero-cost, hands-on cybersecurity career roadmap designed for a 3-machine home lab environment. 
Built around real-world attack scenarios, the OWASP Top 10 (2021), and dual SOC/GRC skill development.

---

## What This Is

This roadmap takes you from zero to job-ready in 6 months using only free, open-source tools. 
No paid subscriptions. No cloud labs. Just hardware you already own and a structured plan.

**Lab Setup:** PC (SOC workstation) + Laptop 1 (attacker) + Laptop 2 (vulnerable target)

---

## What's Inside

- **6-Phase Learning Path** — Foundation → OWASP Attack Lab → SOC Detection → GRC → Threat Hunting → Portfolio
- **OWASP Top 10 (2021)** — Attack, detect, and document every category in your own lab
- **Full Attack Scenarios** — Phishing chains, SQL injection with exfil, brute force + forensics
- **GRC Integration** — Risk registers, policy writing, NIST CSF 2.0 mapping, compliance frameworks
- **Weekly Rhythm** — A daily structure to keep you consistent across 26 weeks
- **Free Certifications & Resources** — No-cost study paths and platforms

---

## Frameworks Covered

`OWASP Top 10 (2021)` · `NIST CSF 2.0` · `NIST 800-53 Rev 5` · `MITRE ATT&CK` · `CIS Controls v8` · `ISO 27005`
